Fish fight

Controls

Fish 1 (left)
  • up : W
  • fire : D
  • down : S (only for combos)
  • back : A (only for combos)
Fish 2 (right)
  • up : UP ARROW
  • fire : LEFT ARROW
  • down : DOWN ARROW(only for combos)
  • back : RIGHT ARROW (only for combos)

Combos

Fire the big bubble : back down back back down fire

Fire the huge bubble : back down back down back back down down fire
